## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Data
We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register for an account
- Use the application
- Contact us for support

**Personal data may include:**
- Email address
- Name
- Company name
- Phone number (optional)

### Financial Data
As a bookkeeping application, we collect and process financial information you provide:
- Transaction amounts and descriptions
- Bank account names (not account numbers)
- Payment methods
- Property names and categories
- Person names and categories
- Receipt images and scanned data
- Income and expense records

### Usage Data
We may automatically collect certain information when you access the application:
- Device type and operating system
- App version and build number
- Crash logs and error reports
- Feature usage patterns (if analytics enabled)

### Photo and Camera Data
- Receipt images captured via camera
- Photos selected from your device library
- Images are processed for OCR (text extraction) and stored securely

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### Primary Purposes
- **Provide Services:** Enable bookkeeping, financial reporting, and data visualization
- **Authentication:** Verify your identity and secure your account
- **Data Processing:** Process receipts via AI/OCR, categorize transactions, generate reports
- **Sync Data:** Synchronize data across your devices and with our backend servers

### Secondary Purposes
- **Communication:** Send important account notifications and updates
- **Support:** Respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
- **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats

### We DO NOT Use Your Information For
- ❌ Selling or renting to third parties
- ❌ Advertising or marketing by third parties
- ❌ Tracking across other apps or websites
- ❌ Creating user profiles for marketing

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

### Security Measures:
- ✅ **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- ✅ **Encryption at Rest:** Server-side encryption for stored data
- ✅ **Secure Authentication:** Token-based authentication with secure storage
- ✅ **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
- ✅ **Regular Updates:** Security patches and updates applied promptly

### Your Responsibility:
- Keep your login credentials secure
- Use a strong, unique password
- Log out on shared devices
- Report any unauthorized access immediately

**Note:** No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Your Data:

**Active Accounts:**
- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- Financial data retained indefinitely for bookkeeping compliance

**Inactive Accounts:**
- Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted after notice
- You can request deletion at any time

**Deleted Accounts:**
- Upon account deletion, personal data deleted within 30 days
- Financial records may be retained for legal/accounting compliance (up to 7 years as required by Thai law)
- Anonymized data may be retained for analytics
